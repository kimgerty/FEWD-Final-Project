Peachy Entertainment is a boutique music and entertainment service house. We offer consultations, booking, management, tour management, publicity, creative marketing, campaign management and advice on both short and long term contracts. If you're not sure what the next step is in your career, just ask!

Founded and run by Cassie Walker, Peachy Entertainment offers a wealth of knowledge about the music industry. As a music industry professional with over ten years experience, Cassie brings her specialist knowledge in management, booking, tour management and journalism. Of particular interest is grass roots development of bands and events. Focusing on getting those as yet unknown talents to the next level in their career is one of Cassie's greatest passions.

Peach Entertainment can help you with:

  • Creative Campaign Consultation & Management
  • Bookings
  • Management
  • Publicity & Marketing
  • Branding
